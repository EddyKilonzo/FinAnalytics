FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is FinAnalytix?

FinAnalytix is an AI-powered personal finance platform built for young Kenyans. Track expenses, set budgets, reach savings goals, and gain deep insights into your money habits — all in one beautiful app.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and in transit. We never sell your data or show you ads. Your data is yours — export it anytime.

How does the AI categorisation work?

Our AI reads transaction descriptions and assigns categories with confidence scores. It learns permanently from your corrections, achieving 94%+ accuracy within a few weeks of use.

Does it work with M-Pesa?

Yes! You can manually log M-Pesa transactions now, and we're actively building direct M-Pesa statement integration. Bank sync is also on our roadmap for later this year.

How quickly can I get set up?

You can be up and running in under 5 minutes. Sign up, add your first transaction, set a budget — and you'll immediately see your dashboard come to life.

How much does it cost?

FinAnalytix is currently free during our beta. We plan to introduce a generous free tier permanently, with optional premium features for power users. No surprise charges.
